RV packing List

Sometimes we just get up and want to go on a road trip. But doing things without careful thought can cause problems. Nobody wants to leave for a roadtrip and not have the essentials. So here are some lists of things to pack when you are going on the road with your rv camper.

This first list is all you need for sanitization and hygiene while camping

  1. Towels
  2. Laundry soap
  3. Tissues, bathroom tissue
  4. Short handled broom and dustpan
  5. Short handled mop/bucket
  6. Rags, shop towels
  7. Large and small garbage bags
  8. Dish cloth, towels, scrub pad
  9. Plastic washing bin
  10. Soap, shampoo, dish soap, laundry soap, pre soak.
  11. Cleaning solution (we use vinegar a lot)
  12. Hand sanitizer, hand wipes

Things you need for campfires and cooking

  1. Cooking utensils
  2. Pans and pots
    3. Coffee pot, teapot
  3. Firewood
  4. Skewers
  5. cooler
  6. Timer
  7. Plastic bags
  8. Hand Towels
  9. Flame resistant gloves (We LOVE the Ove' Glove )
  10. Oven mitts, pot holders
  11. Table cloths
  12. Portable Propane Gas Grill , camp stove, grill for campfire cooking
  13. Fuel for BBQ, stove, charcoal
  14. Plates
  15. Mugs
  16. Cups
  17. Firestarter, fat sticks
  18. Long handled lighter, matches
  19. Any type of newspaper
  20. Weiner sticks, popcorn popper
  21. Other campfire cooking tools
  22. Knives
  23. Spoon
  24. Toothpicks

    25. While you're on your camping trip, it's alway's a good idea to have some

    Do not forget to pack these in the trailer

  1. Dress shirts
  2. Casual shirts
  3. Jeans
  4. Pants
  5. Underwear
  6. Socks/stockings
  7. Undershirts/bras
  8. Sleepwear
  9. T-shirts
  10. Shorts
  11. Dresses
  12. Gloves
  13. Scarves
  14. Umbrella
  15. Laundry kit
  16. Laundry bag
  17. Skirts
  18. Sweaters/sweatshirts
  19. Suits
  20. Swimsuits/cover-ups
  21. Coats/jackets/rainwear
  22. Hats
  23. Leisure shoes
  24. Sandals/flip-flops
  25. Belts
  26. Ties
  27. Hiking/athletic shoes
  28. Snow/rain boots
  29. Dress shoes
  30. Jewelry
  31. Purses
  32. Collapsible totes
  33. Under-clothing document holders

    These Miscellaneous items will come in handy

  1. Cell phone
  2. memory card
  3. List of medications
  4. Emergency contacts
  5. Laptop/tablet
  6. Electronics chargers
  7. Voltage adapter

    8. Good things to have for first aide

  8. Antibacterial wipes/gel
  9. Bug Repellant
  10. Prescription Medication
  11. Travel-size Tissue Packet
  12. Digestion Tablets
  13. Upset Stomach Medication
  14. Allergy Pills
  15. Contact Solution
  16. Pain Relievers
  17. Band-aids
  18. Motion Sickness Tablets
  19. Travel Size Sewing Kit
