Crystal Hot Springs

Location: 8215 UT-38, Honeyville, UT 84314

Admission and parking fee here are free so take your whole family to enjoy this location.

Found in Honeyville, and only about 89 minutes from Salt Lake City and 30 minutes west of Logan, the Crystal Hot Springs is known as the world's biggest natural hot spring and gives us all the options of both hot and cold pools for soaking and swimming.

Crystal Hot Springs ended up being an enterprise in the year 1901 and has actually been offering a location for visitors to delight in ever since that year. Crystal Hot Springs is really distinct as it is among just 2 locations on the planet that have the cold and hot springs less than 50 feet apart from each other. By blending the 2 spring's, soakers delight in the optimum soaking temperatures in each of the swimming pools.

The location is open all year and consists of 3 jacuzzi-type hot tubs, a big soaker swimming pool, a cooler freshwater pool, 2 water slides and a swimming pool. The temperature levels in the swimming pools vary from 65 to around 134 degrees and that depends on the swimming pool you pick.

The springs are a great retreat the entire family can genuinely take pleasure in. They are very relaxing for us grownups, enjoyable and interesting for the more young at heart. The Crystal Hot Springs has lovely and clear mineral pool waters that are hot enough to ease stiff or aching muscles.

But what exactly are hot springs

Hot springs are water bodies that are heated up by geothermal heat, heat that comes from the Earth's center. In volcanic locations, water might touch really hot rocks that were heated up by the magma close by. Hot springs that are active in many volcanic areas might produce water that is superheated. This superheated water is so hot that anyone who dares take a dip in it can lead to some form of injury or worse death.

Policies and Rules

There are some rules and policies that have to be followed while you visit this hot spring. Here are some of them

  • Alcohol is not allowed
  • Glass containers not allowed
  • You are not allowed to barbecue
  • Family friendly swimwear only
  • Wristbands must be worn at all times
  • If there is any type of lightening flash within 30 miles of the springs, the spring will close.
  • Only one person may slide down the slides at a time.
  • Food from the outside are not allowed
