Berkeley Springs State Park

2 S Washington St, Berkeley Springs, WV 25411

Found in the center of the historical Berkeley Springs, The Berkeley Springs State Park has a mineral spa that has been used for many many years. The park is well-known for its great spring water, which streams at a consistent temperature level of about 74.3 degrees. The park's Main and Old Roman and bathhouses provide many choices of day spa services, consisting of massages, baths, showers and saunas.

Berkeley Springs State Park Spa

Treat yourself to a glamorous whirlpool or a joyous bath at the most popular 'Main Bathhouse'. You can likewise delight in a large choice of conventional and trending health spa services. The personal walk-in Roman Bath includes 750 gallons of mineral rich water that is warmed to 102 degrees and comes right out of the spring. The whirlpool is likewise warmed to 102 degrees and consists of a 150-gallon mineral bath that also has jets.
