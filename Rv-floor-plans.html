Rv and Trailer Floor plans to consider for your next rv.

Before purchasing any type of towable rv, the first thing toi consider is what type of vehicle you will be using to tow your rv. You have to make sure that this vehicle is able to tow the weight of the rv. Do not ask questions on forums or people on the internet for advise, always check the owners manual of your vehicle to see the towing capabilities.

If you are the type of person who likes everything being simple, then a small rv will fit your needs perfectly. Below is a list of rvs and their floor plans ranging from small to large.

1. 2018 Airstream Tommy Bahama 27FB

This rv is 27 feet long and comes equipped with many amenities for the average rver. The wooden floor has Anti-wicking Outside Edge Floor Sealant and has complete Aluminum Interior Walls and Ceiling. For your coats and keys, there are hooks. There is an automatic rain sensor and a fantastic fan. The Cabinetry is of Handcrafted Italian Lite-Ply. The interior lights are equipped with a dimmer for evefyone who doesn't like lights being at full brightness.

2. 2018 Airstream Tommy Bahama 27FB

3. 2012 CrossRoads Cruiser Patriot CF345RF

This is a 2 axle trailer that is 33.92 feet in length and is more than able to sleep six people comfortably. Equipped with a Smoke Detector, Carbon Monoxide Detector, Propane Alarm and more than one Emergency Exit. You will also find many options in this trailer like a microwave oven, a refrigerator and a living area in the rear.

There is a satellite and one radio to use as entertainment or to gain information on different channels. To enjoy this entertainment, there is a surround sound system. There is also a DVD player that accepts 1 disk at a time.

The air conditioner on this rig is automatic and also the heater. The heater is able to hold 10 gallons at a time which is the same as 37.9 litres. You will need an electrical hookup or a propane hookup to operate the heater and that is a major plus as you have options.

4. JAYCO JAY SPORT CAMPING TRAILER

This popup trailer is actually one of my favorites. Whenever i have the extra funds to purchase one, i will. The Jay Series camper comes with standard kitchen equipment such as a 35-lb. ice box, Pre-plumbed appliances for propane, Acrylic sink and faucet and a Carry-out dinette table.

Some of the exterior equipment include but not limited to Bumper-welded steel construction, Entrance door access with roof lowered, 12" C-range tires, Corrosion-resistant frame, Domed, seamless fiberglass roof, DuraTek water-repellent sectionalized tent and a 120V G.F.C.I. protected exterior receptacle.

5.2019 AMERICAN COACH PATRIOT

These are some of the most expensive types of recreational vehicles coming in at the high price of $148,940. This price is met with the luxury that Mercedez is known for. The equipped 3.0 V6 engine puts out 188HP and is also Turbocharged.

If you purchase one of these vehicles, you will get a 3 year roof warranty. The length of this vehicle comes in at 24.25 ft. (291 in.) a Width of 6.92 ft. (83 in.) and a Height of 9.67 ft. (116 in.). If you plan on towing any other type of vehicle with this rig, then you can be assured that it will easily tow any vehicle with upwards of 5000lbs. In the kitchen there is a stove and a refrigerator.

There is one bathroom and one bedroom which will sleep 2 people comfortably. Whenever you need to take a shower there is one equipped with curtains to protect the floor from spills.

Conclusion

We have gone over a few rv floor plans to consider for your next purchase. We have gone over a popup camper and even a class b type rv. The options are there for you to choose from. Do the necessary research before making a very important decision like purchasing an rv.
