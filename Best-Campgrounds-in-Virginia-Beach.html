Best Campgrounds in Virginia Beach: Ultimate Guide.

Camp in the friendly atmosphere at First Landing State park or the popular Virginia Beach KOA Campground. Virginia beach has a lot to offer to all campers.

  1. First Landing State Park



    2. The First Landing State Park is the perfect place for a getaway for you and your family. It has many amenities to offer and many different types of accommodations to fit your style. You have the option of choosing to stay at cabins, regular camping, or yurts.

    Cabins

    There are a total of 20 cabins available for rent. Most cabins here are made of cinder block along with cedar siding and carpet and tile floors. The cabins that are made up of wooden frames and hardwood floors are cabins 1 through 6. Cabins are just a easy walk away from the beach which makes them perfect for beach-goers.

  2. Virginia Beach KOA

    3. If you are looking for Virginia camping sites, this is the place for you.

    Lodging
