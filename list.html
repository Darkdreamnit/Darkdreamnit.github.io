Essentials you need for your trip

RV packing List

Sometimes we just get up and want to go on a road trip. But doing things without careful thought can cause problems. Nobody wants to leave for a roadtrip and not have the essentials. So here are some lists of things to pack when you are going on the road.

  1. Towels, face cloths
  2. Laundry soap
  3. Tissues, bathroom tissue
  4. Short handled broom and dustpan
  5. Short handled mop/bucket
  6. Rags, shop towels
  7. Large and small garbage bags
  8. Dish cloth, towels, scrub pad
  9. Plastic washing bin
  10. Soap, shampoo, dish soap, laundry soap, pre soak.
  11. Cleaning solution (we use vinegar a lot)
  12. Hand sanitizer, hand wipes
  1. Portable Propane Gas Grill , camp stove, grill for campfire cooking
  2. Fuel for BBQ, stove, charcoal
  3. Cooking utensils (flippers, grabbers, stirrers etc.)
  4. Pot, pan, griddle
    5. Coffee pot, teapot
  5. Firewood
  6. Firestarter, fat sticks
  7. Long handled lighter, matches
  8. Newspaper or other non-glossy paper (or Fatwood )
  9. Weiner sticks, popcorn popper
  10. Other campfire cooking tools
  11. Skewers
  12. Flame resistant gloves (We LOVE the Ove' Glove )
  13. Oven mitts, pot holders
  14. Table cloth, weights or clamps
  15. Plates, bowls, glasses, mugs, go cups
  16. Knives, forks, spoons
  17. Knife, knife sharpener (or self sharpening)
  18. Toothpicks
  19. Mixing bowl
  20. Bottle/can opener, corkscrew
  21. Ice Chest , Ice
  22. Timer
  23. Plastic containers, zip lock bags
  24. Foil, plastic wrap, wax paper
  25. Napkins, paper towels
  26. Can and bottle cozies
  27. Wine glasses
