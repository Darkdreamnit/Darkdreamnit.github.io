Camping World Locations across the United States

Camping world is a company involved in selling recreational vehicles (rvs), rv parts and providing rv services. You can also purchase camping supplies from this company. The company is located in Lincolnshire, Illinois and became a publicly traded company in October 2016 by raising $251 million in an IPO.

The company operates 120 retail/service locations across 36 states. Below is a list of the Locations you can visit.

Who owns Camping World?

This company is well known around the world but one thing a lot of people do not know is who actually owns it. It is co-owned by a private equity firm known as Crestview Partners alongside Marcus Lemonis.

Sleeping overnight at Camping world.

When the company just started out, it used to allow Rvers to park and stay overnight in their parking lots. But since it shifted to being more of a RV dealership they have gradually moved away from that policy.

However, there are some locations that allow rvers to stay overnight in their parking lot. The ones that do still allow it are usually the ones that have no gate or fence surrounding the premises. The ones that do not offer overnight parking have put up fences and gates to prevent rvers from parking overnight.
