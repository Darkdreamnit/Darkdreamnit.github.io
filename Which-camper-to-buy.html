Trailer buying help just for you

Most people want a simple life just as long as they can provide for their family. But what if you want something a little different. Say for instance, a Recreational vehicle? Here we will go through which campers best fit your lifestyle. Maybe a small rv camper is what you're looking for to get started. I will start off my list with one of the smallest campers on the market.

The 2020 Jayco Eagle 355MBQS

This camper will appeal to those who want something simple but want it to be fancy at the same time. The eexterior on this camper has an alum inum frame that is vacuum bond laminated gel-coated fibreglass exterior walls. It has the strongest roof in the industry, which is the Magnum Truss Roof System and this comes with one piece seamless Diflex II material. It is equipped with the I-class cambered, structured steel I-beam frame.

Lighting is provided by the JaySMART LED Lighting, LED exterior lighting and High gloss, fibreglass front cap with built-in specialty LED lighting. This camper is equipped with 2 propane bottles to keep you cooking for a long time. The se bottles are 30 lb bottles. Trying to get into any rv camper can cause problems for many people, but this camper is equipped with a sturdy, antislip aluminum entrance. It is made in the USA and also has blue LED lights equipped so it is easier to see where you're stepping. Getting on the roof will be easy because there is a ladder attach. On the back of the camper you will find the very useful electric stabalizer jacks.

The interior has wonderful hardwood slide fascia and handcrafted and glazed doors drawers and trim. The floor decking is made of 5/8 inch tongue and groove joined plywood. The carpet will be hard to get stained because it is non other than the shaw residential carpet pad with R2X stain guard. Sound dampening is provided through the carpet by again Shaw residential carpet pad with R-value The average selling price of this masterpiece comes in at around $67,735.00.
