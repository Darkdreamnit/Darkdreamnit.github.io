The Omni Homestead Resort

Address: 7696 Sam Snead Highway, Hot Springs, Virginia 24445

You will experience the hospitality of the south and sophisticated appeal that have actually made The Omni Homestead resort a premier location for 23 U.S. presidents. The resort has over 2,000 acres of picturesque Virginia landscape and supplies classic conveniences and access to a selection of interesting activities.

Known as a luxury resort, The Omni Homestead is surrounded by the four-season elegance of the Allegheny Mountains. This Landmark invited its very first visitors many years prior to the American Revolution, and Princes and Presidents are amongst the visitors who have actually stayed at this historical resort.

A holiday at The Omni Homestead is differentiated by a historical environment, abundant and special customs, and an apparently unlimited variety of outside and indoor activities consisting of 2 champion golf courses, shooting club, tennis, horseback riding, treking, archery, fly fishing, falconry, carriage trips, and mountain cycling. The Omni Homestead spa was refurbished and now consists of unique adults-only Serenity Garden and the Aqua Thermal Suites. Winter season activities consist of snowboarding, tubing, ice skating, mini snowmobiles.

There is a 2 acre, family-friendly, water location known as Allegheny which includes 2 400-ft lazy river, 100-ft water slides, pool for the family, jacuzzi and a regular swimming pool. In 2003, a mini golf course was opened along with The Mini Cascades.

The Omni Homestead continues to provide great lodgings, thoughtful service, and unique Southern beauty. The resort has 483 rooms for guests which consists of 88 large suites together with a range of fine and casual dining restaurants.
