What is an R-Pod Camper?

In the ‘80s, a camper hit the market and had it’s way with everything from kids to bikers. But as the years went on, different RVing styles and different brands of RVs got released in the market, and soon enough the R-Pod camper entered the market.

The R-Pod is a camper that is made by the well-known company Forest River RV which was founded in 1996 by Peter Liegl. Liegl’s aim in starting his company was to create what he considered a better RV that would help people experience the joy of the outdoors.

How much does an R pod cost?

The price of an R-Pod will depend on many factors such as the floorplan, the options you choose, the dealer, and location you get your r-pod from. However, you can expect to pay anywhere from $16,500 for bottom of the line new r-pod all the way up to $36,000 for the top of the line r-pod.

How to choose an R-Pod Camper?

There is no doubt that R-Pod has brought in an entirely new age of car camping. R-Pod comes with everything you need to enjoy your car camping, which includes an R-Pod camper, a roof extension, all kinds of accessories so you have everything, from a roof box to a shower, tent, sleeping area, food storage.

Choosing an r-pod is a big deal and you want to know that the decision you make is the best one. Here are some factors you need to consider.

  1. Does size matter

    2. When choosing which r pod is best for you, you will not only want to decide on how good it looks, you will also want to decide on the size. This has to do with the interior and exterior size. You have to take into consideration the size vehicle you will be using to pull it and also how many people will be staying in it.

  2. Are there any damages (Aesthetically)

    3. On the inside of a r pod camper, take a stroll on the flooring and also the edges with your bare feet. It can be an excellent means to look for damages. See to it that you inspect the cupboards as well as various other storage space locations for damages too.

    Prior to purchasing, check out the breaks, tires, axels, back bumper. be sure to check the front towing capacity also. Likewise examine the seals and also locks on the doors along with the electrical connections and hookups.

  3. Are there any damages (Mechanically)

    4. Are all the systems working? Systems such as the water, electrical, mechanics, and sewer systems are essential.

  4. Draining System Type

    5. There are different types of draining systems in R pods that can lead to different types of problems. A drainage system that you have to turn and twist can sometimes lead to unwanted clogs, while a straight shoot system will have less issues.

  5. Tow Weight

    6. Before buying any r pod camper you should know the weight of said camper and also the towing capability of your tow vehicle.

  6. Repair Budget

    7. The best way to be prepared for anything you purchase in life is to think about having a budget for the repairs on the item you bought. When you buy a r pod camper, try to have a budget set aside for any repairs that may be needed. This is one of the reasons why checking for any damages before you purchase one comes in very handy. You do not want to buy an r-pod camper today then have to do repairs tomorrow.

Is an R-pod worth it?

An R-Pos is worth it if you want a small camper or travel trailer. However, if you need lots of space or have a large family then the R-pod is not for you.

Conclusion

An R-Pod is for those who like to travel small. This small camper allows you to camp in almost any type of location that would be difficult for other recreational vehicles such as Class A Motorhomes. Doing the proper research into the type ov r pod camper you want to purchase is key in getting what will make you happy for years to come.
